About Us

Founded by Kelly Hathaway, Sockeroo’s goal is to exceed clients’ expectations by developing and executing marketing strategies that are compelling, valuable, and measurable.

What’s in a name? The name “Sockeroo” represents both our intent and our approach to client service. “Sockeroo” is defined as “a notable success”, which is our ultimate goal for each client, as well as individual projects.

“Sockeroo” was popular vernacular in the 1940’s, a period of time that is credited with perseverance, personal responsibility, duty, honor, and faith. These are the fundamental characteristics that guide our client relationships and work ethic.

Sockeroo is not a traditional marketing or advertising agency. First, our team possesses extensive experience on the client side of the equation. We understand and appreciate your goals and requirements from a third-party partner because we have walked in your shoes. We are also unique in that we offer clients executive-level expertise to either supplement your inhouse marketing department or fill a more substantial gap. We offer different types of working relationships including monthly retainers, project pricing, and hourly rates. Sockeroo will work with you to assess your needs and develop a relationship that makes sense for your individual business.
